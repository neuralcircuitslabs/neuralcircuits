About

Neural Circuits LLC applies scientific and computational methods to complex systems. We design instruments, models, and algorithms to measure, analyze, and interpret signals from neural activity to quantitative decision systems.

Mission

We reveal patterns in complexity, integrating measurement and computation to turn data into understanding.

Research

What We Do

We measure, model, and analyze complex systems to reveal hidden patterns. Our focus spans neural interfaces, data acquisition, sensors, modeling, simulation, and system optimization.

We combine advanced instrumentation with computational methods to make high-dimensional data interpretable. Our methods include custom instruments, algorithms, scientific computing, and system optimization.
